Deenly – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Deenly is a free Chrome extension that provides an Islamic new tab dashboard including prayer times, Quran ayah, duas, and Hijri calendar. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Deenly does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data with us or any third party. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

Location (Geolocation)

Your device's location is used solely to calculate local prayer times via the public API at api.aladhan.com. Your coordinates are sent directly from your browser to that API and are never stored on any server we control or shared with any third party beyond what is necessary to retrieve prayer times.

Local Storage

Deenly caches prayer times and Quran ayah data in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) to reduce network requests and improve performance. This data never leaves your device.

Notifications

If enabled, Deenly displays browser notifications as dhikr reminders. No notification content is transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party APIs

Deenly makes requests to the following public APIs:

  • api.aladhan.com – for prayer times and Hijri date
  • api.alquran.cloud – for Quran ayah text

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle API requests.

Children's Privacy

Deenly does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository or contact us at the email associated with the Chrome Web Store listing.
